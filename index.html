Environment

  • Before the Rapture, the city stood as a monument to human harmony. Towering spires gleamed beneath endless blue skies, rivers of clean water cut through gardens and plazas, and the streets were filled with laughter, prayer, and shared purpose.
  • Technology and faith coexisted peacefully, providing comfort, abundance, and safety. Families lived without fear of tomorrow, believing the world had finally reached a perfect balance between progress and devotion.
  1. After the Rapture, the same city became a burning scar upon the earth. The skies darkened and split with fire, buildings collapsed into rivers of ash and flame, and the streets once filled with life echoed only with screams and silence. Demons roamed freely, faith was twisted into fear, and survival replaced hope as humanity’s only goal. What was once a paradise became a battlefield, and every remaining soul lived beneath the shadow of damnation.
The Infernal Church

Once a sanctuary of faith and unity, the Infernal Church now burns as a monument to corruption. Sacred halls are cracked and blackened, pews lie shattered, and a blazing inverted cross hangs where prayers were once offered. Demonic altars pulse with stolen power, and twisted creatures lurk among the ruins, drawn to the lingering echoes of worship that still haunt the air.

The Hellgate

The Hellgate marks the place where the world finally broke. A colossal demonic arch rises from scorched stone, its center a swirling inferno that breathes heat, ash, and whispers of damnation. Chains bind the gate to the earth as if reality itself is struggling to hold it closed. Endless waves of demons pour forth, making this passage one of the most dangerous thresholds left in the world.

The Demon Tower

At the heart of the ruined city stands the Demon Tower, a jagged spire of obsidian and fire that pierces the blood-red sky. Rivers of molten rock cascade from its heights, illuminating the battlefield below. This is the seat of the demon in command, where fallen angels lie broken at its base and elite guardians patrol relentlessly. To reach the top is to challenge the very force driving the Rapture itself.

Story

  • In secret and desperation, the cult performed a forbidden ritual meant to defy the Rapture itself. Through blood, fire, and sacrilege, they summoned a human capable of consuming demon blood without being destroyed by it. Their goal was not power or dominion, but survival. They believed this child of ritual could stand against the demons in command and end the Rapture, saving what remained of humanity from annihilation.
  1. The summoned warrior is revered by the cult as the final guardian of the human race. To them, he is proof that humanity has not yet been abandoned, a weapon forged in darkness to fight a greater evil. As the Rapture seeks to cleanse the world of the unworthy, he stands as the sole chance for survival, carrying the burden of every life that still draws breath. In his hands rests the fate of those deemed unfit to be spared.

Gameplay

The gameplay is quick paced hack and slash. you run through rooms collecting various items and upgrades that make you stronger as well as having the option to consume the blood of demons you kill. However, you need to be careful, the right combination of blood will make you overwhelmingly powerful, but the wrong combination could sap your power and make you sluggish. In between runs you stay at the cult's bunker. There you can trade, grow your power and increase your blood tolerance through research. 

The Fallen Angel

Once a divine guardian, the Fallen Angel was cast down during the opening moments of the Rapture. Its wings are scorched and torn, burning eternally with hellfire, and its halo has twisted into jagged metal. Though corrupted, it still wields remnants of holy power, now warped into devastating attacks. The Fallen Angel is a tragic embodiment of faith turned against itself.

The Infernal Priest

The Infernal Priest is a twisted cleric who willingly embraced damnation during the Rapture. Draped in blasphemous robes and crowned with infernal symbols, it channels dark fire and forbidden rites to empower demon hordes. These priests sacrifice souls to strengthen Hell’s grip on the world, serving as both commanders and conduits for demonic power.

The Hellhound

Bred in the depths of Hell, the Hellhound is a relentless predator fueled by fire and rage. Its massive body burns from within, leaving scorched ground in its wake as it hunts in feral packs. Driven purely by instinct and hunger, Hellhounds tear through anything that crosses their path, overwhelming prey with speed, strength, and savage brutality.
